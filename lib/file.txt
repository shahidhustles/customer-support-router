
# **Smart Water Bottle - Product Documentation**

## **1. Product Overview**  
The **Smart Water Bottle** is an intelligent hydration tracking device designed to help users maintain optimal hydration levels. It syncs with a mobile app to track water intake, sends reminders, and monitors temperature. Ideal for fitness enthusiasts, professionals, and anyone looking to improve their hydration habits.

---

## **2. Key Features**  
### **2.1 Hydration Tracking**  
- Tracks daily water intake and syncs with a companion mobile app.  
- Provides personalized hydration goals based on weight, age, and activity level.  

### **2.2 Smart Reminders**  
- LED indicator and vibration alerts to remind users to drink water.  
- Customizable reminder frequency via the mobile app.  

### **2.3 Temperature Control (Pro Model)**  
- Maintains water temperature for up to 12 hours (hot/cold).  
- Displays real-time temperature on the LED screen.  

### **2.4 Bluetooth Connectivity**  
- Connects with iOS & Android devices via Bluetooth.  
- Allows data syncing with fitness apps (Apple Health, Google Fit, Fitbit).  

### **2.5 Battery & Charging**  
- Long-lasting rechargeable battery (up to 10 days per charge).  
- USB-C charging for quick and convenient recharging.  

### **2.6 Leak-Proof & BPA-Free**  
- Made from high-quality, BPA-free materials.  
- Durable, lightweight, and travel-friendly design.
